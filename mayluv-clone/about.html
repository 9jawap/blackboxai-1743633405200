Our Journey

2015 - The Beginning

Founded by Maya Johnson in her kitchen, MayLuv started as a passion project creating small-batch skincare for friends and family.

2017 - First Retail Partnership

Our products debuted in 10 local boutiques, marking our transition from home business to professional brand.

2019 - National Expansion

Launched our e-commerce platform and secured distribution in major retailers across the country.

2021 - Sustainability Commitment

Transitioned to 100% recyclable packaging and achieved carbon-neutral certification for our production process.

2023 - Today

Serving over 500,000 customers worldwide with our expanding range of clean beauty products, while maintaining our commitment to quality and sustainability.
